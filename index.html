About Us

The Phrase “Walk A Cause" itself speaks about the paramount Entity of society. Walk a Cause is an excellent initiative that aims to unite youth from different sections of society for witnessing different societal issues. we wish to raise awareness about the issues that affect our society. Walk A Cause celebrates the Spirit to bring the change in society. Let’s join our hands together and be a part of this initiative.

Our Vision

The vision of Walk a Cause is to create a society with zero tolerance for crimes against women, prioritizing gender equality as a fundamental principle. Our commitment extends to ensuring accessible quality education for all, fostering a future where every individual, regardless of gender or background, has equal learning opportunities. Moreover, we advocate for climate action, making it a top priority to contribute to a sustainable and eco-conscious community. We also champion good health and wellbeing, recognizing the interconnectedness of individual health and societal wellness. Through nationwide walking events and advocacy efforts, Walk a Cause strives to build a world where gender equality, quality education, climate action, and good health are tangible realities for every citizen of India. We envision a future where every person feels safe, empowered, and supported, breaking down barriers for an inclusive and harmonious society, where these core principles are upheld and celebrated in every aspect. Together, we aim to create positive change and a community that embraces these values

Our Mission

The mission of Walk a Cause is to inspire and motivate people across India to come together and support various social, environmental, and health-related causes. Through our nationwide walking events, we aim to raise awareness about important issues and encourage individuals to take action in their communities. Our goal is to foster a culture of empathy, compassion, and active participation in making a positive impact on society. We believe that by uniting people from all walks of life, we can create a ripple effect of change that will benefit the entire nation.
